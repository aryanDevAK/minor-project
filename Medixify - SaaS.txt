Title - Medixify

Description - Medixify is an advanced healthcare management platform designed to streamline hospital operations, enhance patient care, and ensure data security. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as AI, OCR, and blockchain, Medixify integrates hospitals, medicine distributors, and testing labs under one cohesive system. This unified platform eliminates the inefficiencies of paper-based processes by introducing digital prescription writing with OCR and NLP capabilities, ensuring accurate and quick digitization of handwritten notes.
